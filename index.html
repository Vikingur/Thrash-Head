Medical Gear Solid

  • High Concept: Sneak into a hospital, and extract a vital organ from a key patient, set to become the prototype for the next generation of augmented supersoldiers.
  • Controls:

    Move around the map with the arrow keys. Interact with Spacebar. Don't get seen!

  • Player Experience Goals: Feeling stealthy and feeling pressure from all around.
  • Genre: Stealth game
  • Game play: Sneak around Our Lady of Shadow Moses Hospital and dodge the watchful eyes of Dr. Liquid and his GENOME interns.
  • Prototype goal: Make an avoidance stealth game with some light exploration and puzzle solving. Try using the phone at the desk!
  • Features:
  • Setting/Premise: The surgery wing of Our Lady of Shadow Moses Hospital, recently acquired by head surgeon, Dr. Liquid.
  • Story: I can't make sense of it myself, but there's probably nanomachines in the patient's kidney that are important for something...
  • Target Audience: Sneaky, thoughtful, cerebral types.
  • Play time: Around 5 minutes.
  • Strategies: Keep track of where guards are patrolling and looking. Try to find ways to distract people.
  • Prototype Assets: Thrown together in Piskel. "EasyStar" pathfinding forked on Github, somewhat edited and patched in by me.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Okay, I misunderstood this question the last few prototypes. I thought it was asking for which of the Phaser examples it had been drawn from. It's pretty clearly an MSX Metal Gear clone. Top-down sneaking action.
